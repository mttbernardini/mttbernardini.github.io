Hi, I'm a guy from Santa Marinella, Italy. I graduated in 2015 at the «Galileo Galilei» Scientific High School from Santa Marinella. I then received in 2019 the bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering at Roma Tre University. I'm looking forward to attend a master degree in Computer Science.

Programming

I started my path in middle school in web development, learning HTML in 2006, thanks to Windows Live Spaces, and PHP in 2009 to make my first contact form for my first blog (now discontinued). I then delved into JavaScript and CSS in 2011 and made my first web-app during high school, a simple chat-room.

Attending the university course in Computer Science and Engineering, and thanks to more practice, I'm now also proficient in C, Java, Python and C#. I also studied network protocols, database design and software design (with an introduction to the Agile method).

I'm a proud Debian GNU/Linux user since 2013. In 2017 I was selected for the CyberChallenge.IT competition. In 2019 I participated in my first publication regarding the blockchain technology, which was the theme of my BSc thesis.

Music

I started with the piano in 2009, then I got into drums in 2013. I can also play a bit the electric guitar and the bass guitar, mainly for composing.

I don't think to fit on a specific genre because I like composing with different sounds and styles: piano solo is the most obvious one, but I also compose from rock stuff to EDM and Hip-Hop stuff, while experimenting with minimal stuff.

Since 2013 I've been getting used to produce with a DAW. Since 2017 I'm working with Benny Di Guardo as co-producer and manager. I'm also helping my cousin Dankint in the production and creative process.

Two artist that I really enjoy (also as persons) are Fabrizio Paterlini and Jack Conte. Other artists from my music library include: Infected Mushroom, The Glitch Mob, Digitalism, Miracles Of Modern Science, Ludovico Einaudi, Yann Tiersen, Silent Strike, Pomplamoose, Evanescence.

I'm distributing my music mainly through Bandcamp, but some albums may be found on the main digital stores too (iTunes, Google Play, Amazon, Spotify, etc...).

Video making & photography

The last thing I really like doing is videos. I started in middle school, getting the first prize at the contest "Ciak si guida" 2010 of Quattroruote, with the short movie "Quando il giallo" (When the yellow...), made in a school lab. Then, in high school, I met Gisella Ianiri and I assisted in the making of two professional documentaries, "Tre Sguardi" and "Lavori in corso" (this one has English subtitles). I also helped making the official video of "I Just Can't Stop Loving You" by Greta's Bakery.

The quality jump was possible also because I learned how to use a DSLR. I also had a chance to direct my own animation short movie "Big Yellow Taxi", with the help of three friends, for a school project by UNESCO.

In my spare time I like producing musical videos for my friends, under the name of rh productions.
